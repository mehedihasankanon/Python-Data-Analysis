# Mathematical 
- inline math  
    > $y = mx + c$ 

- superscript 
    - $x^{2a+c}$, $y^3$

- subscript 
    - $x_{2a+c}$, $y_5$
- display math 
    $$
    x^2+y^2 = z^2  \\
    x_1 + x_2 > 20 
    $$ 
- fraction
    $$ 
    \frac{a}{b}
    $$ 
- summation 
    $$
    \sum_{i=1}^{n}i  
    $$
- product 
    $$
    \prod_{i=1}^{n}i
    $$ 
- matrix 
    $$
    \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 2 & 3 \\ 
    4 & 5 & 6
    \end{bmatrix}
    $$
- Greek letters 
    - $\alpha$
    - $\beta$
    - $\gamma$
    - $\delta$
    - $\theta$ 
- sqrt 
    $$\sqrt{\sum{x_i^2}}$$
